# jsnielsen.dk — Personal Portfolio Site

Jonathan S. Nielsen's freelance portfolio site. Its job is to convince prospective clients that Jonathan is a strong senior developer worth hiring for an engagement.

## Language

**Client**:
The site's target audience: a company or agency evaluating Jonathan for a freelance engagement on a complex web application.
_Avoid_: Employer, recruiter, visitor

**Engagement**:
A freelance contract with a client company; the unit listed under Work Experience and sourced from `cv-data.json`.
_Avoid_: Job, position, gig

**Case Study**:
The site's primary proof unit: a narrative about one engagement or project covering the problem, Jonathan's role, key decisions, and outcome.
_Avoid_: Work item, selected work entry, portfolio piece

**Service**:
A senior offering listed on the homepage (app modernization, product development, design systems, CMS & content platforms). Each service mirrors at least one case study.
_Avoid_: Generalist menu items (website development, app development, automated testing)

**Positioning**:
The one-line promise the site leads with: senior fullstack developer helping teams build and modernize complex web applications.
_Avoid_: "Immersive digital experiences" (deprecated framing)
